Dr. Sunil Kumar Sharma

Dr. Sunil Kumar Sharma, born on January 6, 1978, in Morang District, Nepal, is a prominent figure in both the medical and political spheres. He is married to Indira Baral. He was born to Mr. Loknath Neupane and Mrs. Huma DeviÂ Neupane.

Political Career

Transitioning into politics, Dr. Sharma was elected as a member of the Federal Parliament of Nepal, representing Morang Constituency No. 3. His election reflects the trust and support he has garnered from his constituents.

Healthcare Initiatives

Dr. Sharma has been instrumental in establishing and supporting medical institutions that provide affordable healthcare services. His efforts have focused on enhancing access to quality medical care, particularly for underprivileged populations.

Educational Contributions

Recognizing the importance of education, Dr. Sharma has contributed to initiatives that offer scholarships and educational resources to students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. These programs aim to empower youth through education, fostering opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Community Development

Beyond healthcare and education, Dr. Sharma has supported various community development projects. These initiatives include infrastructure development, sanitation programs, and disaster relief efforts, all aimed at improving the overall quality of life in his constituency.

Through these philanthropic activities, Dr. Sunil Kumar Sharma has demonstrated a commitment to the well-being and advancement of his community, reflecting his dedication to service beyond his professional and political roles.
